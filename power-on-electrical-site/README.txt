# Power-On Electrical & Air Conditioning QLD Website

## Quick start
Open `index.html` in a browser.

## IMPORTANT (Form submissions)
This website is wired for a form service (Formspree) but uses a placeholder endpoint:

https://formspree.io/f/your_form_id

### To enable form emails:
1. Create a Formspree form and copy your endpoint (looks like https://formspree.io/f/abcdwxyz)
2. Open `contact.html`
3. Replace both occurrences of `https://formspree.io/f/your_form_id` with your real endpoint.

## Gallery photos
- Put your images in: `assets/gallery/`
- Update the `<img src="...">` filenames + captions in `index.html`
